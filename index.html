Initiative/Innovation

As a 3D modelling lover, creating sophisticated 3D models demonstrates my innovative skills. I have been using SketchUp tool for more than 6 years and have used it to create 3D models such as real-world architectures, airplanes, and other items, and upload them to 3D Warehouse, a 3D model database for worldwide users to share their 3D models and download the model they are interested in.

For example, my airplane model has been downloaded for more than 2 thousand times, and other items like an advanced bathtub has been downloaded for more than 5 thousand times. As for the real-world buildings, by applying through Google Building Maker, they are now existing in Google Earth at its geo position.

From these experiences, I understand innovation is not merely have a creative idea, but also requires hard work to make it a real stuff and promote it so that others can see your work, which is personal initiative. This attitude is vital in the field of software engineering to develop innovative products.

Teamwork/Project Management

In my first year of high school, I developed collaborative and project management skill by participating in Student Information Technology Conference (SITCON) Hackathon Camp. During the hackathon event, my group and I developed a plugin for reveal.js (an existed online presentation tool). Our plugin called SlideTrek, it can synchronize audience’s slide page with presenter’s slide page, and simultaneously indicate presenter’s pointer position on audience’s page.

Link to Github Repository

Being a group leader, in order to finish the whole project within 4 days, I had to allocate multiple tasks to group members who were competent to finish them and collaborate closely with each other. Finally, we completely developed our prototype before the deadline and had a successful presentation introducing and demonstrating its features.

This is a clear example demonstrating my collaborative and project management skill which is fundamental in the field of software engineering.

Presentation Skills

I was the leader of Website Designing group in the Computer Science Research Club in high school. My responsibility was planning and teaching website designing courses for junior club members. In this experience, I developed presentation skills including making clear and exquisite slides, intelligibly illustrating the course contents, and answering audiences’ questions.

After two semesters, some proactive club members who were concentrate in class and actively participated in club activities were able to create their own websites. The presentation skills I have expanded in this experience, will be helpful when I have to illustrate the features of the product and explain how it works while working in the field of software development.

Perseverance & Determination

Throughout the 3 years in high school, I participated in a student based Christian fellowship, we went to elementary schools in rural areas every semester to help children with special needs, and children who relatively have fewer resources compared to those in city. I have become more patient and perseverant when facing challenges after the experience.

Being the small group leader of the service-learning group, I had to face some restless children who often run away from the group and unwilling to participate in the activities. Since our goal is to help them to understand love and learn what it feels like to be cared about, it is my responsibility to follow them when they run away, and patiently told them the reason we want them to join the group and play with other children. Surprisingly, after I keep trying again and again to show my care of them, some children who had been keep running away were able to sit down and shared what they had learned throughout the day at the end, and even expressed their gratitude for our patiently teaching and accompanying.

In the field of software engineering, debugging is the process of keep testing and amending the code to achieve high performance and low error rate. By showing my perseverance and determination of achieving the goal in this experience, I am confident the never-give-up attitude will be my advantage in software development.
